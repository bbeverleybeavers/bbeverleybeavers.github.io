Kids Club

A Junior Badminton Club is held on a Thursday evening for 10 - 16yrs. This runs on a Thursday (term-time only) 6.30pm - 8pm at Beverley High School between September and April.

For more details contact Ali Kemp on 01482 860835 / 07812 396277 or email here.
