About the Club

The club is for social or competitive play, so if you are not an experienced player*, or have not played for some time, it is not important.

We play club nights on Thursday's (term-time only) at Beverley High School and with the league teams playing home games on Monday's at Longcroft School Beverley.

Come along and try it for a few sessions, Guest play is £4 per session for adults and £3 for under 18's (Max 3 sessions).

For more details contact Ali Kemp on 01482 860835 / 07812 396277 or email us here.

ERCAS Accredited

 

* Sorry but we cannot accept beginners as courts are in use at all times and because of this there is no time available for coaching.
