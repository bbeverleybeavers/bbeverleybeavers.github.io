Club Nights

Our main club night is on a Thursday evening from 7pm to 9:30pm at Beverley High School during term time.

Please note that courts are in use at all times on a club night and because of this we cannot accept beginners as there will be no courts/time available for coaching.

The High School is opposite Asda and the Durham Ox pub. Parking is on Norwood. The sports hall is reached through the pedestrian gates, then right under the covered walkway and across the playground and staff car park (the car park is closed on an evening) to the sports hall.

During the league season (Oct to Apr) we also have courts available at Longcroft School on a Monday evening 7pm to 9pm where our league teams play their home matches.

Depending on which teams are playing there are usually 1 or 2 courts available though there will be some nights where there will be no courts available as 2 teams will be playing.
